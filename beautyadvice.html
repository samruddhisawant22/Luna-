Fenty Beauty's Soft Matte Foundation Kept My Skin Shine-Free..!


written by BIANCA LAMBERT UPDATED JUN 11, 2021

Fenty By Rihanna

Fenty Beauty states that the foundation allows you to “get a perfectly smooth, natural-looking finish that lets skin look like skin” They also add that they created this formula “with grape seed oil and sodium hyaluronate to make a foundation that normal and dry skin types will love”.I really recommend Fenty Beauty’s hydrating foundation. It has an exceptional natural finish, builds up easily to a medium to full-coverage, is ideal for different skin types [I have a combination skin] and is long-lasting!


MY QUICK + EASY 5 MINUTE MAKEUP ROUTINE.


written by EMMA WHITE UPDATED JUN 11, 2021

5 minute Makeup routine .

I start out my five minute makeup routine with a tinted moisturizer (see my top tinted moisturizer post). A tinted moisturizer offers coverage that will blend onto the skin to hide flaws yet look natural. It is also hydrating so it is forgiving. You don’t have to be perfect with this like a foundation, it can be quickly applied and blended in with the fingers. A few really good tinted moisturizers to try include Laura Mercier Tinted Moisturizer, bareMinerals Complexion Rescue and NARS Pure Radiant Tinted Moisturizer.
